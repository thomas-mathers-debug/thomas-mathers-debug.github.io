My approach to problem solving

This week I got blocked by the use of arrows (=>) in functions. To solve this problem I used a mixture of google and console.logging. Googling lead me to useful information such as example code and youtube videos while console.logging let me test what I was doing and answering my questions. This is my go to problem solving method when it comes to codding and I think its one of the best, asking coach and peers is something that I would do as a final option.
